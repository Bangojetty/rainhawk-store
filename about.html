Our story

Founded on a soggy trailhead

Rainhawk started the way a lot of good ideas do — cold, wet, and a little stubborn. After one too many trips cut short by gear that quit when the weather did, we set out to build kit that performs when conditions stop cooperating. Today we design weatherproof shells, sun hoodies, packs, and trail essentials in Colorado and test them everywhere the map gets interesting.

Sustainability

Built to last, sourced with care

Disposable gear is the enemy. We use recycled face fabrics, responsibly sourced down (RDS-certified), and PFC-free water repellents wherever performance allows. Every product is designed to be repaired, not replaced — and our 2-year warranty backs that up.

Warranty

The 2-year weather warranty

If your Rainhawk gear fails from a defect in materials or workmanship within two years, we'll repair or replace it. Plus 60-day returns on anything that isn't right for you — no interrogation required.
