Privacy Policy

Last updated June 5, 2026

Rainhawk Outdoor Co. ("Rainhawk", "we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

When you place an order we collect your name, email, shipping address, and order details. Payment card data is collected and processed solely by our payment processor (Stripe) — it never touches our servers and we never store full card numbers.

How we use it

  • To process and fulfill your order, including sharing your shipping address with the supplier who ships your item.
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and respond to support requests.
  • If you opt in, to send occasional marketing emails — you can unsubscribe any time.

Who we share it with

Only the service providers needed to run the store: our payment processor (Stripe), our fulfillment/supplier partners (to ship your order), and our email provider. We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies

We use essential browser storage to keep your cart between visits. Any analytics or advertising cookies, if added later, will be disclosed here and gated behind your consent where required.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing support@rainhawkgear.com. We honor applicable rights under GDPR and CCPA.

Contact

Questions? support@rainhawkgear.com.
