Shipping & Returns

How your gear gets to you, and what happens if it isn't right.

Shipping

Where & how fast

We ship across the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and Australia. Most orders are processed within 2 business days and arrive within 5–12 business days depending on destination, since items ship directly from our sourcing partners to keep prices honest.

  • Free standard shipping on orders over $75.
  • Flat $7.95 standard shipping under $75.
  • You'll get an email with tracking as soon as your order is on its way.

Returns

60-day, no-interrogation returns

If something isn't right, return any unworn item in its original condition within 60 days for a full refund of the item price. Start a return by emailing support@rainhawkgear.com with your order number and we'll send a prepaid label for domestic returns.

Warranty

2-year weather warranty

Every Rainhawk product is covered for two years against defects in materials and workmanship. If it fails, we'll repair or replace it — just reach out with a photo and your order number.
